# Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis

Data-driven strategy to optimize customer targeting and campaign ROI for 45k+ marketing leads.

### 📊 [View Live Interactive Dashboard on novyPro](https://www.novypro.com/create_project/bank-target-marketing)

### Executive Q&A
- **Top KPIs:** Total Conversions, Conversion Rate, Avg. Balance, and Contact Frequency.
- **Target Segment:** Management & Technicians (no housing loans) due to higher conversion and balances.
- **Strategy:** Cap outreach at **3 calls** and peak spending in **May** to capture seasonal deposit highs.

### Technical Highlights
- **Tools:** Power BI, Power Query.
